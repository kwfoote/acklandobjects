Yes, this image of me was carved in stone because I wanted it to last for eternity! This is actually my tombstone. In second-century Palmyra, where I'm from, the deceased were buried in underground tombs filled with niches, or alcoves. The niche was then sealed with a bust-length relief carving of the deceased.
