Very good observations. These people are working outdoors. They are harvesting cranberries on the island of Nantucket. Even though there was a commercial market for cranberries in this period, picking them had not yet been mechanized. People still went out to the marshes and used their hands to comb the berries off the plants in order to collect them. Johnson was interested in capturing the rural American way of life that was quickly disappearing due to industrialization and urbanization.
